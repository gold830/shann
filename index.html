About Us

MR DJ SHAN (real name Nalugoda Shaban) is a dynamic Ugandan DJ, producer, event host, and music proprietor whose passion for music has shaped an inspiring journey in East Africa’s entertainment scene.

Born and raised in Lugazi, a small town along the Kampala–Jinja Road, MR DJ SHAN began his musical journey in 2000, driven by a deep love for sound, rhythm, and the power of music to bring people together. What started as a passion soon evolved into a professional career that would take him from local events to the heart of Uganda’s vibrant nightlife.

By 2005, he had made his way into the Kampala music scene, performing in a variety of clubs, bars, and major events. His energetic performances, versatile music selection, and natural ability to read crowds quickly earned him recognition among partygoers and event organizers alike.

MR DJ SHAN later joined Firebase Entertainment as their official events DJ, gaining valuable experience working alongside some of Uganda’s most influential artists and entertainment professionals. During this time, he honed his craft, built his reputation, and expanded his influence within the industry.

Today, MR DJ SHAN stands as an independent DJ and music entrepreneur, known for delivering electrifying performances, producing engaging mixes, and hosting memorable events. With decades of experience behind the decks, he continues to push boundaries, entertain audiences, and contribute to the growth of Uganda’s music culture.

From Lugazi to the big stage, MR DJ SHAN’s story is one of passion, persistence, and the relentless pursuit of musical excellence.

Career

During his reign as a top events, club, and occasions DJ, MR DJ SHAN built a strong reputation performing during prime time in some of Kampala’s most popular nightlife spots. His performances lit up major venues including Club Volts, Club Ambiance Masaka, Flyzone Kajjansi, Nyondo, Cyclone, and several other bars such as Happy Boys, where he consistently drew large crowds with his energetic sets and versatile music selection. As his audience grew, he expanded into media and entertainment partnerships. In 2015, he joined Bukedde TV under Gong Records, and later in 2017 moved to BBS TV, where he delivered outstanding DJ content and entertainment programming for over eight years. In November 2024, he announced his departure from the station, and in early 2025 he joined XFM under Vision Group, where he now focuses on promoting Reggae and Dancehall, genres that remain central to his show.

Throughout his career, MR DJ SHAN has also shared stages and collaborated with renowned regional talents, including celebrated Kenyan DJ Simple Simon, the late MC John Maina (MC Fullstop), and DJ Juan during their first visits to Uganda. His skills behind the decks have earned him significant recognition in DJ competitions, winning over seven DJ battles and holding five consecutive Club DJ Awards up to 2015. Among his major accolades are Stakes Best Deejay of the Year (2013–2014) and UG Maestro Best DJ (2017) after highly competitive battles. Today, while he rarely participates in DJ battles, MR DJ SHAN continues to be invited as a guest performer, showcase DJ, or judge, reflecting his respected status in the industry.

Throughout his career, MR DJ SHAN has proven his creativity not only behind the decks but also as a visionary event curator. Since 2013, he has successfully hosted and headlined his signature annual event, “The MR DJ SHAN Mixtape Party,” a high-energy showcase that ran until 2024, bringing together music lovers, creatives, and top talents from across Uganda’s entertainment industry. Each edition was staged at exciting venues such as Freedom City, Kenlon Royale Hotel Mengo, Climax Entertainment Hub Makindye, and Vox Lounge Makindye, where audiences experienced his electrifying DJ sets and collaborative performances. In 2025, he rebranded the event into a new concept titled “Dancehall Mu Reggae,” a show dedicated to the powerful fusion of Dancehall and Reggae—the genres that define his signature sound. Designed to grow beyond a single concert into a global movement with multiple activities and activations, the show reflects MR DJ SHAN’s mission to deliver world-class performances while cementing his reputation as one of Uganda’s most respected Reggae DJs.

MR DJ SHAN is a seasoned Ugandan DJ, event host, and music curator known for delivering high-energy performances at some of the country’s biggest stages. Over the years, he has headlined and performed at major events including four AFCON qualifier matches at Mandela National Stadium, the final Uganda show of the famous duo P-Square at the Cricket Oval, the KCCA Carnival, several editions of the popular Roast and Rhyme shows, and Reggae Fest alongside MC Fullstop and DJ Juan, where he also stood out as a charismatic host. His talent has also taken him beyond Uganda, performing at venues such as Club Signature in Kisumu, among others.

MR DJ SHAN remains highly active on the entertainment circuit with regular performances across the country. He performs in Jinja every Friday, takes over the decks at Kenlon Royale Hotel Mengo on Saturdays, and entertains beach crowds at Bunjako Beach in Buwama on Sundays, while also making mid-week appearances in different cities and venues. His consistency, versatility, and passion for reggae and dancehall continue to keep him among the most respected DJs in Uganda’s music scene.
