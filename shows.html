DANCEHALL MU REGGAE

Born from passion, rhythm, and a deep love for authentic sound, Dancehall Mu Reggae is the electrifying signature show hosted by Mr DJ Shan. Launched in 2025 as a bold rebrand of the widely celebrated The Mixtape Parties, this experience marks a new era dedicated to elevating Dancehall and Reggae culture across Uganda...


Driven by his recognition as one of the country’s top Reggae selectors, DJ Shan created this platform to amplify the genre he lives and breathes. The debut edition, held on October 5th, 2025 at Jovia City, set the tone for what is quickly becoming a cultural movement. Powered by KULTURE THE AGENCY in partnership with XFM, the night delivered an unforgettable 99% Dancehall and Reggae experience.

Featuring standout performances from artists like Ziza Bafana, Vyper Ranking, Mad Tiger, Sama Sojah, and Cyza Musiq, the event fused high-energy performances with immersive décor and powerful sound. Partygoers turned up in numbers, vibing through a night that celebrated pure Caribbean influence with a Ugandan twist.

When DJ Shan finally took the stage, he delivered a masterful Reggae set before transitioning into a high-energy Dancehall finale—solidifying his vision and leaving the crowd wanting more.

Dancehall Mu Reggae is more than just a show—it’s a growing movement. With plans to expand into a full series, DJ Shan continues to push boundaries, aiming to make Dancehall and Reggae not just genres, but a dominant cultural wave in Uganda and beyond.

REGGAE SALUTE

Turn up the volume and tap into the culture with Reggae Salute—a vibrant, high-energy radio experience hosted by Mr DJ Shan alongside the unstoppable Thee Epic Major Dukeplaay, one of Uganda’s most electrifying MCs. Since its launch in early February 2025, the show has become a weekly ritual for lovers of authentic sound, airing live every Sunday from 3:00PM to 5:00PM on XFM 94.8, with an extended repeat session every Wednesday night...


Blending the raw energy of Dancehall with the soul of Reggae, Reggae Salute perfectly captures the essence of both hosts—Shan’s smooth selector style and Dukeplaay’s unmatched hype. More than just a radio show, it’s an interactive platform where fans connect directly, diving into real conversations about the culture, the music, and the movement.

From studio vibes to the streets, the show goes beyond the mic—featuring live sessions from hangout spots and ghettos where the real fans reside, bringing the sound straight to the people. Behind this powerful movement is Nicholas Masaba of The Kulture The Agency, whose vision continues to push Reggae and Dancehall to the forefront of Uganda’s music scene.

Reggae Salute isn’t just something you listen to—it’s something you feel. Tune in, lock in, and link up every Sunday, and be part of the wave shaping the future of Reggae and Dancehall in Uganda.

DANCEHALL MU REGGAE FESTIVAL

Get ready for the ultimate evolution of the movement—Dancehall Mu Reggae Festival, trippers' edition. Set against the breathtaking shores of Bunjako Beach, this upcoming experience will take everything you loved about Dancehall Mu Reggae to a whole new level. Hosted by Mr DJ Shan, the visionary behind the brand, this three-day beach festival promises a non-stop celebration of pure Dancehall and Reggae energy...


Born from the success of the original Dancehall Mu Reggae show—launched in 2025 after the rebrand from The Mixtape Parties—the festival carries forward the same mission: to make Dancehall and Reggae a dominant cultural force in Uganda. From the unforgettable first edition at Jovia City, powered by The Kulture The Agency and XFM, to now—this is the next chapter of a growing movement.

Expect bigger stages, louder sound, immersive beach vibes, and a powerful lineup of Uganda’s finest Dancehall and Reggae acts—building on performances from stars like you least expect. Festival-goers can look forward to daytime chill sessions, sunset vibes, and electrifying night performances, all curated with DJ Shan’s signature touch—strictly Reggae and Dancehall.

With the dates soon to be announced, Dancehall Mu Reggae Festival is gearing up to deliver an even more electrifying, unforgettable experience—three days of music, culture, connection, and pure vibe by the beach. This isn’t just an event—it’s a lifestyle.

MR DJ SHAN MIXTAPE PARTIES

A true foundation of the movement, Mr DJ Shan Mixtape Parties is a legendary series that has defined nightlife culture across Kampala and beyond. Hosted by Mr DJ Shan, this iconic run of shows spans over twelve years of unforgettable moments, bringing together massive crowds and unmatched energy in some of Uganda’s top venues—from Freedom City, Climax Lounge, and Kenlon Royale Hotel to D'Fantasy Lounge, Happy Boys, and more...


Over the years, the Mixtape Parties have hosted an incredible lineup of Uganda’s biggest stars, including Spice Diana, Jose Chameleone, Pallaso, Radio & Weasel, Ziza Bafana, Kalifah Aganaga, Aziz Azion, Karole Kasita, Fefe Bussi, Alien Skin, Mesach Semakula, ChOzen Blood, Jowy Landa, Cyza Musiq, Bebe Cool, Fik Fameica, David Lutalo, Desire Luzinda, Grenade Official, Coco Finger, Big Eye, and many more—alongside top MCs and DJs who have graced the stage.

One of the most iconic milestones came during the 10-year celebration at Sky Beach Freedom City, where Bobi Wine made a special appearance, cementing the legacy of the brand. The series reached a powerful climax in 2024 with a standout edition at Climax Lounge Makindye—featuring a red carpet experience, high-profile performances, and an explosive showcase set by DJ Shan himself, sharing the stage once again with heavyweights like Pallaso, Jose Chameleone, Radio & Weasel, and more.

More than just parties, these events became a cultural staple—an unforgettable, unmatched experience that built a loyal following over the years. And now, with the evolution into Dancehall Mu Reggae, fans can expect even bigger, bolder experiences from a legend who has commanded the decks for over 25 years.
